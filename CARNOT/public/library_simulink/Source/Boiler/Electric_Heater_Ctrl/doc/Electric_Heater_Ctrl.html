Electric_Heater_Ctrl
Path: CARNOT/ heat_source/boiler

Purpose
Model of an electrical heater (instantaneous water heater).

Description

The model heats the entering mass flow of the Thermo-Hydraulic Bus with an electric power.  A Thermal Node is used to model the thermal inertia of the heater and losses to the ambient . The model is described by the equation:

           mcp * dT/dt = UA*(Tamb-T) + mdot*cp_in*(Tin-T) + Ctrl*Electric_heating_power

with

  • cp_in : heat capacity of entering fluid in J/kg/K
  • Ctrl   : Control input, range [0...1]
  • mcp  : thermal capacity of the heater in J/K
  • mdot : entering fluid massflow in kg/s
  • Tamb : ambient temperature input in °C (for thermal losses)
  • Tin    :   temperature of entering massflow in °C
  • T     : temperature of the node and outlet in °C  
  • Electric_heating_power in W
  • UA : thermal loss coefficient in W/K

 

Parameters

 

Inputs

  •  Thermo-Hydraulic Bus  
  • electric heating power in [W]

Outputs

  •  Thermo-Hydraulic Bus

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes (all inputs except temperature)
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
