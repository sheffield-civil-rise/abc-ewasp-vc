Electric_Heater
Path: CARNOT/ heat_source/boiler  

Purpose
This block calculates the heat input from electrical heating.

Description

The block symbolises electrical heating. It heats the entering flow which is defined by the Thermo-Hydraulic Bus with electrical power entering the block.

The calculation is performed using the equation for heat input to a fluid:

           T(outlet) = T(inlet) + Pelectric/(mdot*cp)

with:

  • T are the temperatures in in degree Celsius
  • Pelectric is the electric heating power in W
  • mdot is the massflow in kg/s
  • and cp the heat capacity in J/(kg*K)

To avoid a division by zero the massflow is limited to values above 1e-5 kg/s.

A PT1 transfer function with a time-constant of 60 s is used the model the thermal inertial of the electric heater.

 

Parameters
- none -

Inputs

  •  Thermo-Hydraulic Bus
  • Pelectric

Outputs

  •  Thermo-Hydraulic Bus

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes (all inputs except temperature)
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
