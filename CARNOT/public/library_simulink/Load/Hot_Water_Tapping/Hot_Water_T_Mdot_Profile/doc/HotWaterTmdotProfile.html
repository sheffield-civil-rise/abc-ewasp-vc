Hot_Water_T_Mdot_Profile

Path: CARNOT/Load/Hot_Water_Tapping

Purpose
Hot water consumption with a setpoint temperature and massflow profile.

Description
There are different methods to evaluate the hot water comfort (see QAIST 2012).
In this model the hot water draw-off is defined by a setpoint temperature and a massflow. The tapping starts at the specified time with the specified flow-rate. The draw-off ends when the specified duration is reached.
The model includes a mixing valve trying to maintain the setpoint temperature (defined as hot water temperature). The necessary massflow through the cylinder is calculated by this block and set in the outport to the cylinder.  

The model uses the mdot_rep_seq block to create the massflow profile.

The T_cold_Water_Sine block is used to create a seasonally varying cold water temperature.

 

Parameters and Dialog Box

 

Literature

QAIST : Methodology for the Assessment of the Hot Water Comfort of Factory Made Systems and Custom Built Systems, ITW University of Stuttgart, 2012
