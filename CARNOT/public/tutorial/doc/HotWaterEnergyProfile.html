Hot_Water_Energy_Profile

Path: CARNOT/Load/Hot_Water_Tapping

Purpose
Hot water consumption with an energy profile.

Description
There are different methods to evaluate the hot water comfort (see QAIST 2012).
In this model the hot water draw-off is defined by an energy profile (see also DHW_Energy_Tapping block). The energy tapping starts at the specified time with the specified flow-rate. The draw-off ends when the energy is reached.
The model includes a mixing valve trying to maintain the setpoint temperature (defined as an offset on the cold water temperature).

 

Parameters and Dialog Box

 

Literature

QAIST : Methodology for the Assessment of the Hot Water Comfort of Factory Made Systems and Custom Built Systems, ITW University of Stuttgart, 2012
