Thermal Node
Path: CARNOT/Basic/Thermal_Models

Purpose
One node model of a thermal capacity with entering massflow and losses to ambien. This block breaks the direct feedthrough of any incoming temperature vector.

Description
The block breaks the incoming temperature vector and integrates it according to:

mcap * dT/dt = UA_loss * (Tamb - Tnode) + mdot * cp * (Tin- Tnode)

where

  • UA is the loss coefficent to ambient in W/K
  • mcap is the thermal capacity = mass in kg * heat capacity in J/(kg*K)
  • cp is the heat capacity of the entering fluid flow in J/(kg*K)
  • mdot is the massflow rate in kg/s
  • Tamb is the ambient temperature in °C


Parameters and Dialog Box

 

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough  No
Sample time               Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                  No
