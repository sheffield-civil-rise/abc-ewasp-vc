T_cold_Water_Season
Path: CARNOT/Load/Hot_Water_Tapping

Purpose
Create a seasonally varying cold water temperature.

Description
The block creates a cold water temperature over the year. The uses a linear curve between minimum and maximum or an approximation with a sine curve.  

The minimum and maximum values are set to the 15th of the selected month.

Both linear and since curve can have the asymmetric times for minimum and maximum  (e.g. lowest temperature in March and highest in July).
Two different parameter sets are used for the short and the long intervall.

 

Parameters and Dialog Box

Characteristics

Sample time                none
