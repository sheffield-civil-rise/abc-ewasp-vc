alfa_ceiling

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Heat_Transfer

Purpose
Calculate the heat transfer of a ceiling to a room.

Description

Convective and radiative heat transfer at the SURFACE OF A HORIZONTAL CEELING.

The block is disigned to be combined with the Wall_basic block. It is used in the Roof block.

 

Inputs

  • [AIV] : conditions of zone node
  • Ts : temperature of wall surface, °C

Outputs

  • qdots : specific heat flow into wall construction, W/m²
  • [S] : heat and air flow to zone node. none

 

Parameters and Dialog Box


Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from model
Vectorized                   No
