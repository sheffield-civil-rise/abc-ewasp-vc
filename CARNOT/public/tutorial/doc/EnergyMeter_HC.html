EnergyMeter HC
Path: CARNOT/Control

Purpose
The block shows the power, the heating and the cooling energy balance a component.

Description
The block shows the power [W] and energy [J] gain or loss in one  Thermo-Hydraulic Bus (THB) between the inlet and the outlet of one component. Connect THB_in to the THB entering the component and THB_out to the THB at the outlet.
 The block differentiates between negative ("cooling") and positive ("heating") power. The integreation of the power to an energy balance is done separately for heating and cooling.

Inputs:

  • Thermo-Hydraulic Bus at component inlet
  • Thermo-Hydraulic Bus at component outlet

Outputs:

  • thermal power in [W]
  • heating energy in [J]
  • cooling energy in [J]

 

see also: THB format  and examples.

 

Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   Yes
Sample time                Inherited from driving block
Vectorized                   No
