CreateSdat

Path: CARNOT/Basic/Thermal_Models/CreateSdat

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Purpose:
Create the storage data bus (Sdat) for the storage models (Storage_Type_N).

Description:
All relevant informations for further evaluation of the storage are combined in this structure. The parameter volume in the storage mask should be promoted from this block. 

 

 

 

Input:

Tamb : ambient temperature in °C
Internal energy : changes in storage energy content in J
Energy losses : Thermal losses of storage in J
THB inlet pipe 1 : inlet THB of storage port "pipe 1"
THB outlet pipe 1 : oulet THB of storage port "pipe 1"
THB inlet pipe 2 : inlet THB of storage port "pipe 2"
THB outlet pipe 2 : oulet THB of storage port "pipe 2"
THB inlet pipe 3 : inlet THB of storage port "pipe 3"
THB outlet pipe 3 : oulet THB of storage port "pipe 3"
THB inlet hx 1 : inlet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 1"
THB outlet hx 1 : oulet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 1"
THB inlet hx 2 : inlet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 2"
THB outlet hx 2 : oulet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 2"
THB inlet hx 3 : inlet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 3"
THB outlet hx 3 : oulet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 3"
Tnodes : temerature of storage nodes in °C

Output:

Tamb : ambient temperature in °C
Internal energy : changes in storage energy content in J
Energy losses : Thermal losses of storage in J
THB inlet pipe 1 : inlet THB of storage port "pipe 1"
THB outlet pipe 1 : oulet THB of storage port "pipe 1"
THB inlet pipe 2 : inlet THB of storage port "pipe 2"
THB outlet pipe 2 : oulet THB of storage port "pipe 2"
THB inlet pipe 3 : inlet THB of storage port "pipe 3"
THB outlet pipe 3 : oulet THB of storage port "pipe 3"
THB inlet hx 1 : inlet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 1"
THB outlet hx 1 : oulet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 1"
THB inlet hx 2 : inlet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 2"
THB outlet hx 2 : oulet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 2"
THB inlet hx 3 : inlet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 3"
THB outlet hx 3 : oulet THB of storage port "heat exchanger 3"
Tnodes : temerature of storage nodes in °C
Volume : storage volume in m³
StgType : number of Storage Type (1-5)

 

Characteristics:

Direct Feedthrough : Yes
Sample Time : Inherited from driving block
Vectorized : No
