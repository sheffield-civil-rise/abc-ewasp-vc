Heattransfer2Ground_ISO13370
Path: CARNOT/Basic/Heat_Transfer

Purpose
Calculate the heat transfer of a house to the ground.

Description
Calculates the heat transfer of a house to the ground accoring to the ISO 13370 model "slab on ground".

Inputs:

  • none

Outputs:

  • Thermal power to ground in W (negative when leaving the house, positive when entering the house)

Parameters and Dialog Box

 

 


Characteristics
Direct Feedthrough   No
Sample time                Inherited from model
Vectorized                   No
